About this site

Countless public servants across varying levels of government using GitHub every day, both internally and publicly to make government more open and moer collaborative. This site is dedicated to showcasing stories of government using GitHub to empower more collaborative open source, open data, and open government efforts.

Have a question about using GitHub at your agency? Have a great story to tell?
Contact GitHub's Government Team at gov@github.com.
