About this site

At GitHub we love collaboration and we love the things we’ve seen and believe are possible when government makes use of GitHub. We’ve even got someone dedicated to this beautiful relationship, Ben Balter.

Featured content on this site is curated by GitHub. We love reading about what’s going on and capturing stories directly from our government users.

Fork it!

If you’re a government - city, state our country, no matter - or a non-profit or group of civic hackers, fork this page and add your name to the list.
