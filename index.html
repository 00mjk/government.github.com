Get started today

Signup

If you don't alrady have one, sign up for an individual GitHub account that's specific to you.

If you've already got a personal GitHub account, simply add and confirm your government email address.

Dive into best practices

There's a growing, semi-private community of government employees sharing best practices for software collaboration within government.

If government email address is associated with your account and verified, you should have access.

Create an organization

An organization is your official presence on GitHub. Once logged in, create a free organization. You can always upgrade later.

Prefer to host your own GitHub instance? request a free trial to get started.

Talk to a human

Questions? Post a question in the government commmunity to get feedback directly from other GitHub users, or email government@github.com to talk to GitHub's government team directly.
